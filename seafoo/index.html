Seafoo™

A next-gen seafood alternative made from New Zealand-grown mycelium and marine algae oils — crafted for chefs, rich in omega-3s, and designed to heal the ocean with every plant-based, ocean-positive bite.

10% to MVP
Early Stage
The Problem & Solution

Feed people. Heal the ocean.

Problems We're Solving
1

Global demand for seafood is high — and it requires ever-greater removal of marine life from already stressed ecosystems

2

Coastal communities worldwide lack access to sustainable, affordable protein — especially where fish stocks have collapsed

3

Current plant-based seafood alternatives are nutritionally poor — lacking the omega-3s and texture that make seafood valuable

How Seafoo Solves It
1

NZ-grown mycelium provides the texture and protein of seafood without removing a single fish from the ocean

2

Marine algae oils provide the omega-3 profile of wild-caught fish — nutritionally equivalent, ocean-positive

3

Grown from food waste — circular, low-footprint production. Partners: Hikurangi, Cawthron Institute, Patagonia Provisions

Target Markets

Where Seafoo will go

👨‍🍳

High-End Chefs & Restaurants

Premium positioning with top-tier restaurants seeking ocean-positive ingredients. Story-rich product that resonates with sustainability-conscious diners.

🌏

Protein-Scarce Communities

Coastal communities where fish stocks have collapsed need affordable, nutritious protein. Seafoo's scalable production model can address this gap.

🌿

Plant-Based Market

The $7B+ global plant-based seafood market is underserved nutritionally. Seafoo's omega-3 profile differentiates it from soy-based alternatives.

🧪

Nutraceuticals

High-value fatty acid and omega-3 extraction from the production process. Potential pharmaceutical and supplement market applications.
